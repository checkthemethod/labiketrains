Splendid!

Need to get to work in Los Angeles? Wish your commute was super fun instead of a drag?

We provide a rolling party along curated routes run by Conductors – experienced urban cyclists – to educate new riders, harness the safety of riding in a group and kicking it up a notch by making the ride a fun social experience. Plus it’s totally free!

All you need to do is submit your email, cell phone number and the route that you’re interested in. If you don’t see a route that works for you – suggest one!

Our Routes

With 8 routes (including 2 Super Trains: Silver Lake to Santa Monica and MacArthur Park to Long Beach) We've got a lot of ground already covered. Take our Commuter Survey to help us understand when & where the next train should be.

Commuter Survey

Help us understand you & LA better. By letting us know through anonymous data collection where you live and where you work, we'll be able to draw a new map of Los Angeles focused on human powered transportation. It's only 3 questions!
